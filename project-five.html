The Marine Projects Division of pargasiran is active in the design, engineering, and construction of various types of vessels and ships, including support vessels, fishing vessels, tugboats, high-speed passenger crafts, aluminum boats, as well as floating and fixed offshore platforms, and a wide range of heavy steel structures (either fully assembled or as prefabricated modules). In the field of product engineering, the company provides the following technical and engineering services to its clients:
- Feasibility studies based on customer requirements
- Basic design
- Detailed design and advanced calculations in the fields of structural engineering, systems engineering, electrical and electronics, mechanical and HVAC, navigation, and control
- Construction and implementation based on computational analyses and computer simulations

- ساخت و اجرا بر مبنای محاسبات و شبیه سازی های رایانه ای
